# Stakeholder Management & Data Access

## Key Stakeholders
- **Site Management**: Full access to analytics and reports
- **HR Business Partners**: Access to department-level insights
- **Team Leaders**: Aggregated results for their teams only
- **Employees**: Anonymous participation, no individual data access

## SharePoint Access Structure
SharePoint Root/
├── Management/ (Site leadership – full access)
├── HR/ (HR team – anonymised data)
├── Department_Heads/ (Department-specific reports)
└── Templates/ (Read-only for all stakeholders)

## Data Privacy Protocols
1. No individual responses visible to anyone
2. Minimum group size of 5 for any disaggregated reporting
3. Physical register stored securely, destroyed after verification
4. Digital files stored on UK-based SharePoint servers
