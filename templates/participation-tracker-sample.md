# Participation Tracker Sample

## Overview
Real-time dashboard for monitoring survey completion rates across the organization.

## Dashboard Components:
- **Completion Rate by Department** – Visual progress bars
- **Daily Participation Trends** – Time-series tracking
- **Reminder Flagging** – Automated identification of non-respondents
- **Executive Summary** – Key metrics for leadership

## Key Functionality:
- **Real-time Updates** – Live formulas reflecting participation status
- **Conditional Formatting** – Visual cues for completion thresholds
- **Data Validation** – Ensuring response integrity
- **Multi-department View** – Consolidated reporting across teams

## Technical Implementation:
- Dynamic named ranges for scalable data
- `TODAY()` and `COUNTIFS()` for live tracking
- Data validation lists for department categorization
- Protected sheets with editable response fields
